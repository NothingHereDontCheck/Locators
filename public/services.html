New Construction Homes in Atlanta

Through our partnership with The Rainey Property Group, Metro Locators connects buyers with brand-new construction properties across the greater Atlanta metro area. Whether you're purchasing your first home, upgrading your living situation, or relocating from out of state, we guide you through the entire process — from initial search to closing day — at no cost to you as the buyer.

New construction offers advantages that older homes simply cannot match. You'll benefit from modern floor plans designed around today's lifestyles, energy-efficient systems that lower monthly utility costs, and builder warranties that protect your investment for years after move-in. In many cases, buyers who get in early on a development can lock in pre-construction pricing that's significantly below market value by the time the neighborhood is complete.

Atlanta's new construction market is one of the most active in the Southeast. From affordable townhomes in the western suburbs to executive single-family homes in Alpharetta, Milton, and Cherokee County, there's a wide range of options for every budget and lifestyle. Our team stays current on what's being built, what's selling, and what incentives builders are currently offering — so you don't have to.

Pet-Friendly Living

Emotional Support Animal Letters

An Emotional Support Animal (ESA) is a companion animal prescribed by a licensed mental health professional to provide therapeutic support for individuals with qualifying emotional or psychological conditions. Unlike service animals, ESAs do not require specialized task training — their therapeutic value comes from companionship and the comfort of their presence.

Under the Fair Housing Act (FHA), tenants with a legitimate ESA letter have the legal right to live with their emotional support animal even in housing with strict no-pets policies. Critically, landlords and property managers cannot charge pet deposits or monthly pet fees for a certified ESA. In Atlanta's rental market, where pet deposits routinely run $300–$500 upfront plus $25–$75 per month in pet rent, a valid ESA letter can save you $600 to $1,400 or more over the course of a single lease.

Our partner Pettable connects you with a licensed mental health professional in your state who evaluates your eligibility and — if approved — provides an official ESA letter that meets all federal housing requirements. The entire process is completed online and typically takes 24 to 48 hours.

Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the apartment locating service really free?
Yes — completely. Metro Locators is paid a referral fee by apartment communities when a renter signs a lease at one of our recommended properties. You never receive a bill from us, at any point in the process. Our incentives are perfectly aligned with yours: we only get paid when you find a home you love.
Can an ESA letter save me money on rent in Atlanta?
Potentially yes. Under the Fair Housing Act, landlords cannot charge pet deposits or monthly pet rent for a certified ESA. In Atlanta, where pet fees can add $600–$1,400 or more per year to your housing costs, a valid ESA letter from a licensed provider eliminates those charges entirely.
What types of new construction are available in Atlanta?
Atlanta's new construction market includes townhomes, single-family homes, and in some areas, new condo developments. Active price points range from around $280,000 for suburban townhomes to $700,000+ for large single-family homes in communities like Alpharetta, Milton, and Forsyth County. The Rainey Property Group can help you identify options across all of these categories.
How long does the ESA letter process take?
Through Pettable, the online assessment takes approximately 10 minutes to complete. Once submitted, a licensed mental health professional reviews your assessment and — if you qualify — your official ESA letter is issued within 24 to 48 hours.
Do I need to be pre-approved to look at new construction homes?
Not necessarily to begin browsing. However, most builders require a mortgage pre-approval letter before accepting a purchase contract. If you're not yet pre-approved, we can connect you with lending partners who specialize in new construction financing. Starting this process early puts you in the best position when you find the right home.
Can I use Metro Locators if I'm relocating from out of state?
Absolutely — and out-of-state relocators are some of the people who benefit most from our service. Navigating an unfamiliar city's rental market from a distance is stressful. We provide local expertise, virtual tour coordination, and hands-on guidance through the application process so you can secure a home before you even arrive.
