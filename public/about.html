About Metro Locators

We Help People Find Home — Free of Charge

Metro Locators is a free apartment locating service built on a simple idea: finding your next home shouldn't be stressful, expensive, or time-consuming. We do the searching. You just move in.

Our Story

Built to Solve a Real Problem

We started Metro Locators after watching renters — friends, family members, colleagues — spend weeks drowning in outdated online listings, scheduling tours at apartments that had already leased, paying fees for advice that led nowhere, and missing out on move-in specials they never even knew existed.

The apartment search process was broken. Too many renters were navigating it alone, without access to the local knowledge and community relationships that could make the difference between a frustrating search and a great outcome.

Metro Locators was built to fix that. We staff our markets with experienced, locally-rooted apartment locators who know which communities offer the best value, which buildings have the most responsive management teams, and which move-in specials are quietly available to renters who know where to look.

And because apartment communities pay us a referral fee when a renter moves in, you never see a bill from us. Our interests are completely aligned with yours — we only succeed when you find a home you love.

The Process

How Our Free Model Works

Apartment communities across our service areas pay Metro Locators a referral fee when a renter we refer signs a lease. This is a standard, industry-wide practice — the same way a staffing agency is paid by the employer, not the job seeker. You receive expert guidance, local knowledge, and personalized support at no cost to you, ever.

01

You Tell Us What You Need

Budget, neighborhood preferences, move-in date, pet situation, must-haves — we take it all in with a quick intake form. Takes under two minutes.

02

We Search Our Full Network

Your locator searches hundreds of listings — including off-market units and communities with unpublished specials — to surface the best matches for your criteria.

03

You Tour Your Top Picks

We schedule tours at your convenience, coordinate with community leasing teams, and accompany you (virtually or in person) so you have support throughout.

04

We Help You Close the Deal

From application to lease signing, your locator is available to answer questions, flag red flags, and help you navigate the fine print. No pressure, ever.
